Booman, or as he was first called Specimen no 5 was born in a lab in the outskirts of California. While transporting him as a test product to the Amazon forests, the plane crashed,killing everyone but him. Thats how Booman ended up with a sweet family in Wayanad. He grew up to be a powerful man, often showing signs of superpowers. He started using his powers to save the people of his beloved town and thats how Specimen number 5 became Booman. His powers include superstrength, X ray vision, ability to communicate with animals etc.
